Privacy Policy

Effective date: 30 May 2024

MetalliCult ("we", "us", "our") is an independent, fan-made website. We respect your privacy and process personal data in line with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). This notice explains what we collect, why we collect it, and how you can exercise your rights.

Information we collect

  • Device and usage data: pages visited, approximate location (city/region), browser type, and referring URLs captured through privacy-focused analytics.
  • Contact information: email address if you contact us directly.
  • Affiliate interaction data: outbound clicks on affiliate links may be tracked by our partners to attribute commissions.
  • Server logs: basic technical logs used to keep the site secure and investigate abuse.

How we use data

  • Operate, maintain, and improve site performance and content.
  • Respond to inquiries and support requests.
  • Comply with legal obligations and affiliate program requirements.
  • Protect against fraud, abuse, or security threats.

Legal bases (GDPR)

  • Consent for analytics cookies/trackers where required.
  • Legitimate interests to maintain site security and present content.
  • Contractual necessity when processing is required to answer a request you submit.

CCPA notice

We do not sell personal information. California residents may request access to, deletion of, or details about data shared for business purposes. We respond within statutory timelines and will verify identity before fulfilling a request.

Data sharing

  • Affiliate networks and merchants: to track referrals and verify commissions.
  • Analytics providers: for aggregated, non-identifying usage reports.
  • Service providers: email or hosting partners who support site functionality under confidentiality obligations.
  • Legal compliance: when required by law, subpoena, or to enforce our Terms of Service.

Cookies & tracking

We aim to keep tracking minimal. If analytics or affiliate programs set cookies, they are used to understand site performance and attribute purchases. You can adjust browser settings to block or delete cookies, and most browsers offer Do Not Track or Global Privacy Control signals which we honor where legally required.

Data retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes described or to comply with legal and accounting requirements. When data is no longer needed, we delete or anonymize it.

Your rights

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data.
  • Object to or restrict processing in certain circumstances.
  • Opt-out of non-essential cookies and marketing communications.
  • CCPA: request to know categories of data collected and request deletion.

International transfers

When data is transferred outside your region, we rely on appropriate safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses or equivalent protections to keep your information secure.

Security

We use HTTPS and commercially reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data. No method is 100% secure; please contact us if you identify a vulnerability.

Children’s privacy

This site is intended for users 16+ (or the age required by your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Contact

Email: metallicult.team@gmail.com. Please include “Privacy Request” in the subject to exercise your rights.

Updates

We may update this policy and will post the revised date at the top. Material changes will be highlighted where feasible.
