About MetalliCult

MetalliCult is a professional, fan-run hub built to help Metallica devotees shop smarter. We publish transparent guides, curate merch that respects the band’s legacy, and make sure every recommendation meets affiliate and compliance standards.

Meet the curator

Ori Elbaz is a lifelong Metallica fan, gear collector, and the voice behind MetalliCult. After years of chasing tour drops and comparing print runs, Ori started this site to organize the best picks in one place. His background in digital marketing and product research means every recommendation balances credibility, value, and user experience.

Why this hub exists — Fans deserve merch that lasts beyond one wash and arrives from trustworthy stores. MetalliCult spotlights items that pass real-world use tests and come from merchants with solid fulfillment records.

How we choose products — We cross-check fabric quality, print durability, sizing feedback, shipping policies, and return windows. Listings are re-reviewed whenever retailers update stock or prices, and anything that slips in quality gets removed fast.

Transparency first — We rely on affiliate commissions to keep the site online. Links may earn a commission at no extra cost to you, and every page links to our Privacy Policy, Terms of Service, and Affiliate Disclosure so you know exactly how MetalliCult operates.

What we curate
  • Iconic tees, hoodies, and stage-ready layers that respect album art.
  • Drinkware, patches, posters, and collectibles you can display proudly.
  • Seasonal finds like Hawaiian shirts and outerwear for tour nights.
  • Care tips and sizing notes sourced from actual fan feedback.
Trust & compliance
  • FTC-ready affiliate disclosures and clear “no affiliation” messaging.
  • GDPR/CCPA rights summarized with straightforward contact options.
  • Merchants vetted for secure checkout, reliable shipping, and fair returns.
