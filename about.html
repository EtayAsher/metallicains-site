About MetalliCult

@metallicult • Fans of Metallica & Rock

Who we are — MetalliCult is a fan-driven community built by people who live and breathe rock. We celebrate the sound, style and energy around Metallica and the culture that grew with it.

What we do — we spotlight quality gear, timeless styles and standout finds inspired by the music we love. Everything you see here is hand-picked because it looks great, feels right and fits the vibe of true rock fans.

What you’ll find — apparel, posters, collectibles and stories from the road. We keep the page fresh with new drops and refined picks that keep the spirit loud.

What we love

  • Vinyls & rare posters
  • Vintage tees & premium fits
  • Stage energy & tour vibes
  • Design that screams rock
