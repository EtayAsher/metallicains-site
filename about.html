About MetalliCult

MetalliCult is an independent, fan-built destination for Metallica devotees. We curate merchandise, write practical guides, and share news that helps fans gear up with confidence. This site is not affiliated with or endorsed by Metallica or their partners.

Expertise • Authority • Trust

Our roots — We are lifelong fans who have followed the band across decades and formats. From bootlegs to box sets, we know what stands up to real-world use and what belongs in a collector’s stash.

Our process — Every item featured is reviewed for design accuracy, material quality, shipping reliability, and retailer reputation. We choose partners that meet global standards and give buyers clear return options.

Our promise — Transparency first. We publish affiliate disclosures, respect privacy rights (GDPR + CCPA), and avoid any language suggesting official endorsement. If something is out of stock or doesn’t meet expectations, we pull it from recommendations.
