Accessibility Statement

MetalliCult is committed to making our fan hub usable by everyone. We are actively working toward WCAG 2.1 Level AA compliance, focusing on semantic headings, consistent navigation landmarks, descriptive alternative text for images, keyboard-friendly controls, and sufficient color contrast between text and backgrounds. Our current audits prioritize high-contrast black and yellow palettes that exceed 4.5:1 for standard text and bold call-to-action buttons.

If you encounter an accessibility barrier or need content in another format, please contact metallicult.team@gmail.com. We review feedback within five business days and continually improve the site to serve all visitors.
